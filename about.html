Hi, my name is Konrad, currently working as Product Designer in healthcare industry. I focus on accessible solutions that help business grow and provides real value to the users who really need it. By education im a Graphic Designer, I have a keen eye on the typography and really enjoy crafting visuals, but all this is meaningless if what we are creating is not what people want to use - this took me to the place where i am today, where research and tests became my #1.

I have finished Graphic Design education with Bachelor of Arts degree. During the study, I started working as a Brand Designer and continued it for next 4,5 years just to find out that my place belongs in products - crafting data driven, purposeful and user-centric experiences. Transitioning into Product Designer role in 2021 felt as a natural process of my journey - I was actively trying to search for research opportunities and possibilities to validate assumptions in ongoing branding projects for long time already, but it wasn’t the right place for it. Since then, im constantly learning new things, pursuing business and product skills, on a mission to connect business and the users in the most effective way.
